# MacBeat
### The Heartbeat of Your Workspace

**Turn your MacBook into a tactile instrument.** 
MacBeat isn't just an app; it's a new way to interact with the device you use every day. By bridging the gap between digital sound and physical touch, MacBeat transforms your laptop's chassis into a responsive, high-fidelity drum machine.

---

## ⚡️ Physical Magic
Stop clicking buttons and start feeling the rhythm. MacBeat uses **spatial awareness** to sense your touch on the aluminum body of your Mac. 
*   **Top Surface**: Deep, resonant kicks. 
*   **Sides**: Crisp, snappy snares.

It’s ultra-responsive, zero-latency, and feels like magic.

## 🔄 Intelligent Looping
Inspired but don't want to break your flow? Start tapping, and MacBeat's **Rhythm Engine** will follow your lead. 
*   **Auto-Sync**: It intuitively understands your tempo and keeps your loop in perfect time.
*   **Multi-Layering**: Build complex soundscapes by switching instruments on the fly. 

## 🎨 Infinite Sonic Canvas
Your music, your rules.
*   **Signature Sounds**: Add your favorite samples. Whether it’s a vintage drum kit or a found-sound recording, MacBeat gives it a physical home on your laptop.
*   **Dynamic Visuals**: Watch your workspace come alive with real-time feedback that matches your performance.

## ☁️ Invisible Yet Always There
MacBeat lives discreetly in your **Menu Bar**. 
*   **Instant Access**: One click (or one tap) and you're ready to perform. 
*   **Smart Silence**: It stays quiet when you're focused and wakes up the second inspiration strikes.
